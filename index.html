Hello!

I am Vinay, a first-year graduate student in the Computer Sciences Department at The University of Wisconsin-Madison

Previously, I was a Systems Engineer at Hewlett Packard Enterprise RnD Labs and a remote Research Fellow at Systems and Storage Lab, University of Texas at Austin under the guidance of Prof. Vijay Chidambaram.

In the past, I have collaborated with Dr. Kimberly Keeton [Gen-Z persistent fabric-attached memory], Prof. Supreeth Shastri and Prof. Arun Kumar [GDPRBench].

My research interest is computer systems:
Storage, Operating systems, Distributed systems and systems ML.
