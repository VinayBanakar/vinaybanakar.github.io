I work at Google's Machine Learning Performance Team building systems for TPU/GPUs to accelerate AI at scale. I received my Ph.D. from [University of Wisconsin-Madison](https://www.cs.wisc.edu/){:target="\_blank"} [(ADSL)](https://research.cs.wisc.edu/adsl/){:target="\_blank"}, advised by [Andrea](http://pages.cs.wisc.edu/~dusseau/){:target="\_blank"} and [Remzi Arpaci-Dusseau](https://pages.cs.wisc.edu/~remzi/){:target="\_blank"}. My expertise is cross-stack systems optimization: from hardware/software co-design to operating systems and distributed systems.

Previously, I was a Researcher at [Google - Systems Research Group](https://techsysinfra.google/research/){:target="\_blank"}, working with [Kim Keeton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kimberly_Keeton){:target="\_blank"}. I have also held positions as a Research Affiliate at the [Microsoft Gray Systems Lab](https://www.microsoft.com/en-us/research/group/gray-systems-lab/){:target="\_blank"}, a Senior Systems Engineer at Hewlett Packard Enterprise RnD Labs, and a Research Fellow at [Systems and Storage Lab](https://utsaslab.cs.utexas.edu/){:target="\_blank"}, [University of Texas at Austin](https://www.cs.utexas.edu/){:target="\_blank"}.
